It's a mental game A positive mindset: you can go the distance

What most people don't realize is that running is a mental game. Whether you think you can or think you can't run a certain distance or speed ultimately determines whether you can or not.

It's almost impossible to not fall in love with running as you achieve goal after goal. That's why it is so important to start small and be consistant in your runs. Remember that comparison is the theif of joy - start where you are, and compare YOURSELF to the person that YOU were yesterday.
